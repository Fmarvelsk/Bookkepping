Introduction

The purpose of cybersecurity is to help prevent cyberattacks, data breaches and identity theft and can aid in risk management. When an organization has a strong sense of network security and an effective incident response plan, it is better able to prevent and mitigate cyber-attacks.

Software incubation hub

As part of our contribution to the global community, we train young novices into experts in the various software design and engineering patterns and techniques, accross different languages and platforms.

Internet and Web Security

We sensitize and train members of the University community and Nigeria as a nation about current trends in security threats and vulnerabilities, and especially how we can collectively mitigate them.
